Opportunity to Grow

Get the chance to learn how to develop your idea for business. Our course will take you how to prepare your business. Starting from idea, researching your idea, prepare the business plan, assembling your team and managing the business.

Experienced Mentor

We bring together experienced enterpreneur to be your mentor. Learn from their problems, solutions, and mindset to bring the business together. You can also enrich your network with them and have a chance to collaborate with each other.

Get Funded

Have a problems with money for your ideas? We provide continuous funding support to our participants on our events. Furthermore, we also have an event for demonstrate your business to funders for more advanced investment.
